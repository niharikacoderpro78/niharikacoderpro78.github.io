Project: Blinkit Sales & Inventory Performance Analysis

Project Overview

This project analyzes Blinkit’s sales and inventory performance using Power BI. The objective was to evaluate revenue trends, outlet performance, product demand, and operational metrics to support data-driven business decisions.

Business Objective

To identify top-performing products, evaluate outlet-level sales efficiency, understand purchasing patterns, and highlight areas for operational improvement.

Key Analysis Performed

  • Analyzed total sales, average sales, and product distribution
  • Evaluated outlet performance based on size, type, and location tier
  • Identified high-demand product categories
  • Compared revenue performance across different outlet segments
  • Built interactive KPI dashboards using DAX measures

Tools & Technologies

  • Power BI (Dashboard & Visualization)
  • DAX (Calculated Measures & KPIs)
  • Data Cleaning & Transformation
  • Excel / CSV Data Sources

Business Impact

The dashboard provides comprehensive visibility into revenue trends and operational performance, enabling stakeholders to identify profitable segments and areas requiring strategic improvement. This analysis supports informed and data-backed business decisions.
